About Us

Mission

Build an easy to use and transparent data storage platform that provides insights on lifestyle areas (physical, mental and social) that are specific to users.



Mission (Layman's Terms)

WSH aims to leverage technology used by social media companies to collect data to build a data repository that can be used to give users personalized feedback on their life style instead of selling them stuff.

One questions many of us frequently asking myself is why we have more energy some days than others. An Oura ring tells you that you didn’t sleep well. Ok, well you probably already knew that. But why didn’t you sleep well. What were some of the factors that contributed to that and are they avoidable? Some might be obvious, but others might not be as much.

Leveraging technology used by existing technology/social media companies, wearables and other sources WSH aims to build platform that will offer users transparent control of their data and provide feedback that will improve quality of life across all aspects of life – physical, mental and social.



Vision

What Sticks Health (WSH) is a health data storage infrastructure that provides personalized feedback. It is a work in progress. You can visit it at https://what-sticks-health.com to see what is there so far but here is a breakdown of what is going on. If you think you can help feel free to reach out.

Existing Platform

  • Data Storage: web application that allows users to upload Polar Beat training session, weight and user specified activity ( i.e. drinking coffee, headache, emotion, etc.,)
  • Feedback: Polar Beat training session indexed to model for cardio health

Next Steps (Immediate)

  • Data Storage: iOS and Android device application (weight and activity) https://github.com/costa-rica/whatSticksKivy09
  • Feedback: build correlations to provide simple feedback — similar to the training session heart rate modeling

Next Steps (Near Future)

  • Encrypted data storage
  • Anonymized data
  • Interoperability to upload and share data
  • Build more in platform feedback
  • Include well established health benchmarks to assist user feedback
